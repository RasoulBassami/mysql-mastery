# Part 1: MySQL Fundamentals & Key Concepts

-----

Welcome to the first section of `mysql-mastery`!

Here, we aren't just repeating basics. We aim to deepen our understanding of the "WHY" behind every database design decision. These fundamentals are the bedrock upon which all advanced knowledge is built.

  > Learn why InnoDB is almost always the right choice and understand concepts like Transactions and Row-Level Locking.

* **[Lesson 2: Data Types & Optimal Selection](./02-Data-Types/README.md)**

  > Understand the impact of choosing `TINYINT` vs `INT` at scale and proper usage of Date, String, and JSON types.

* **[Lesson 3: Relationships, Keys & Joins](./03-Keys-And-Relationships/README.md)**

  > Hands-on with the backbone of relational databases: Primary Keys, Foreign Keys, and various JOIN types.
